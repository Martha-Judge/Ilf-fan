There is more to a donkey than dressing them up and thinking they are cute, they are actually highly intelligent creatures with many interesting characteristics. Here are some juicy fun facts to get your eyes on...

  • Their massive ears have different uses. Not only can they hear beautiful brays from 60 miles away, they control body temperature by releasing heat.
  • They can have up to 44 teeth. You wouldn't want to be gnash gnashed on by them.
  • The donkey has been used as a working animal for atleast 5000 years :(
  • Some donkeys are impressively hairy - take a look at this bad boy.
